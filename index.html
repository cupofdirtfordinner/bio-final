WHY IT'S SPECIAL

WHAT DO THEY EAT

Mostly seals, sometimes fish.

WHAT IS THEIR ROLE IN THE ECOSYSTEM

regulating seal and fish populations.

WHAT OTHER ORGANISMS WILL BE AFFECTED IF POLAR BEARS GO EXTINCT

Fish populations will decrease, because of how polar bear populations heavily regulate their food chain.

WHAT ARE ADAPTATIONS THAT MAKE POLAR BEARS UNIQUE

Their translucent fur appearing as white and allowing them to camoflauge while still absorbing as much heat as possible due to their black skin. Their great tolerance to frigid temperatures also allows them access to the world's arcitic regions, capitalising on the large untapped fish populations.

THREATS

WHAT ARE THE THREATS / WHY ARE THEY ENDANGERED

Largely, global warming is responsiblem due to melting all the ice in the polar bear's heavily ice-reliant habitat.

WHO IS RESPONSIBLE FOR THE DECREASE IN NUMBERS / IS THERE ANY CRIMINAL ACTIVITY
